2021 Great Britain Road Safety

Situation

The UK Government's Department of Transport publishes reports on Road Safety on an annual basis. These reports are supported by the release of datasets containing the information related to:

  1. Road accident details in the calendar year
  2. Vehicles (involved in road accidents)
  3. Casualties (related to road accidents)

Task

Supplied with the 2021 data files, the task was to conduct exploratory data analysis on the information contained in these files and to compare the data analysed to the official 2021 annual report.
Important headline metrics to be reviewed were:

  1. Road fatalities and KSI
  2. Casualties by severity and month
  3. Vulnerable road users
  4. Fatalities by type of vehicle
  5. Casualties by age and sex
